About

This website was created by a fan, for the fans. Bringing together all the most important news about the sport we love. I aim to share, in an impartial and unbiased way, the latest events and stories that will mean the most to those who give the most to the sport...the fans.

Footballfinity is about football and only football, be it virtual or in the real world. No matter what club you supprt we are all connected by a love of the beautiful game.

Although based in the UK, the focus of this site is global. Football itself is a global sport and interesting news and stories can come from anywhere.
